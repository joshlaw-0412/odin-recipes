Lemonade

Description:

Lemonade is a sweetened beverage made from lemons, sugar, and water.
It is popular in the United States during the spring and summer, when it is generally served chilled with ice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (250 ml/8.5 oz) fresh lemon juice
  • ¾–1 cup (160–225 g/5.6–7.9 oz) white or turbinado sugar
  • 5–6 cups (1.2–1.5 L) water

Steps:

  1. Combine the lemon juice and sugar in a pitcher or jug. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Stir in the water.
  3. Chill and/or serve over ice.
